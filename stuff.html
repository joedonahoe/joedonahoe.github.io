Tableau Conference 2015 Schedule Planner

Tableau conferences are only 2.5 days long. They also have a ton of sessions. That can make it tricky to fit all of the sessions you want to go to. Some things are relevant to your day job, some things are presented by celebrities in the data visualization community, and some things are just interesting. To help straighten it all out, I built this planner.
